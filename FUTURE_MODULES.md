# Future Modules

## Future Phase - Video Localization & Media Studio

Status: Proposed future phase. Not implemented.

### Purpose

Add a professional media localization studio for video translation, subtitles,
voice-over, dubbing, synchronization, QA, and localized export.

### Included Features

- Automatic Speech-to-Text.
- Subtitle Management for SRT, VTT, and ASS.
- Subtitle Translation.
- AI Voice-Over.
- AI Dubbing.
- Audio-Video Synchronization.
- Localized Video Export.
- Media Localization QA.
- Workflow Integration.

### Expected Workflow

1. Import video or audio.
2. Generate or import transcript.
3. Create or import subtitles.
4. Translate subtitle segments.
5. Apply terminology and semantic QA.
6. Generate optional voice-over or dubbing.
7. Synchronize subtitles, audio, and video.
8. Review and approve localized media.
9. Export localized video and subtitle files.

### Professional Constraints

- Human review remains required for final approval.
- AI-generated voice-over and dubbing must be explainable and reviewable.
- Subtitle translation must respect timing, readability, terminology, and
  semantic fidelity.
- All media localization actions must be auditable.
- This module must not be implemented until it is promoted from Future Phase.
